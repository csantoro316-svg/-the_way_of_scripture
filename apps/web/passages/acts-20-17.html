Context

Acts 20:17 should be read within the flow of its chapter and the larger argument of Acts.

Common Interpretations

One group of men. Called elders. Made overseers. Told to shepherd. Three words, one office, one function — caring for people, not ruling a rank. Peter makes the same connection: Elder, shepherd, overseer — again the same men, the same task, described three ways. Not a hierarchy. A job, seen from three angles: age and maturity (elder), responsibility for watching over souls (overseer), and the actual daily work of tending people (shepherd).

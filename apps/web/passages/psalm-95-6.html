Context

Psalm 95:6 should be read within the flow of its chapter and the larger argument of Psalm.

Common Interpretations

Restored reading: "Come, let us bow down and fall prostrate; Let us kneel before Yahweh our Maker." Shachah appears alongside "bow down" and "kneel" — not redundant phrases but reinforcements of the same truth: worship is a posture of the whole self. The people do not stand and observe — they fall and adore. Worship here is not passive; it is participatory.

Passage

Philemon 1:23-25

Additional Greetings

Berean Standard Bible

Epaphras, my fellow prisoner in Christ Jesus, sends you greetings, as do Mark, Aristarchus, Demas, and Luke, my fellow workers. The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be with your spirit.

Context

The closing reminds the reader that this appeal stands within the shared life of gospel laborers, not merely an isolated exchange between two men.

Common Interpretations

Paul ends by surrounding the letter with names, fellowship, and grace. The reconciliation he seeks is not abstract. It belongs inside the actual network of believers serving Christ together. The final word is fitting: grace. That is the atmosphere of the whole letter, and without it the appeal Paul makes would collapse.

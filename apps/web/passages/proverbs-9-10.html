Passage

Proverbs 9:10

LSB

"The beginning of wisdom is the fear of Yahweh, And the knowledge of the Holy One is understanding."

Context

Proverbs 9:10 should be read within the flow of its chapter and the larger argument of Proverbs.

Explanation

Sebomai is a reverent awe that prepares the soul to receive His holy truth.

Restored reading: "...in vain do they hold Me in awe..."

A Living Study And Exchange

This site is a living study effort. Thoughtful feedback is welcome, and future study may lead to clarification, correction, or revision.

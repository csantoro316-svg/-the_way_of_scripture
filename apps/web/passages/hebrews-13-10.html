Context

Hebrews 13:10 should be read within the flow of its chapter and the larger argument of Hebrews.

Explanation

To rebuild any of these four in physical form was to misunderstand what the new covenant had accomplished. This framework helps explain why the early church's rejection of sacred buildings was not poverty or circumstance — it was theological conviction.
